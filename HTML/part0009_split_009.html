Pain aux noix et yaourt

Walnut and yoghurt bread

This bread is quick and easy to make and is good with cheese or simply lashings of salted butter.

1 loaf

360g plain flour

140g wholemeal flour

1 tbsp bicarbonate of soda

1 tbsp baking powder

100g chopped walnuts

½ tbsp honey

200g plain yoghurt

1 tbsp vegetable oil

Mix all the dry ingredients together in a bowl. Put all the wet ingredients in a separate bowl and whisk, then combine the wet with the dry and mix well for 3–4 minutes. The dough should be fairly wet. Preheat the oven to 220°C/Fan 200°C/Gas 7.

Put the dough into a 1kg non-stick loaf tin. Place it in the oven and immediately turn the heat down to 200°C/180°C/Gas 6 and bake the loaf for an hour or until golden and fully cooked. Take the loaf out of the tin and leave to cool on a wire rack.
