Ingrid's Mandelkuchen

 

For the cake:

·         1 cup sugar

·         4 eggs

·         2 cups flour

·         2 tsps. baking powder

·         1 cup liquid whipping cream

 

For the topping:

·         2 sticks butter

·         1 cup sugar

·         2 cups sliced almond

 

Mix sugar and eggs until fluffy.  In a different bowl, blend flour with the baking powder.  Add the flour mix to the eggs, alternating with the whipping cream.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Cover a 13 x 9 cookie sheet with parchment and spread the dough on it.  Bake for 10 minutes.  Remove from the oven.

 

While the cake is baking, prepare the topping.  In a frying pan, melt the butter and sugar.  Add the almonds and stir until the almonds are lightly brown.  Be careful, they will burn faster than you think.

 

Spread the almonds mix on the cake and bake another 10 minutes.  Turn off the heat and let the cake sit 20 more minutes in the oven.
