Catalan Cream


This is basically a crème Brulee, but in Spain is familiar by the other name Catalan cream. Smooth and creamy with caramelized sugar crust.

Serves: 4

Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup fine sugar
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Directions:

1. Combine the milk and vanilla in a saucepot. Add lemon zest and cinnamon stick.

2. Bring to boil and reduce heat to medium: simmer for 4-5 minutes and discard the cinnamon and lemon zest.

3. Beat the egg yolks and cornstarch until smooth. Pour the egg yolks into a milk, in a steady stream whisking entire time.

4. Pour the mixture into ramekins or serving dishes and pop in the fridge for several hours.

5. Sprinkle the cream with sugar and preheat the broiler. Broil the cream for few minute until the sugar is caramelized.

6. Remove and serve.
