Vanilla Crescents with Almonds

 

This recipe comes from Liesel, who owned a restaurant known as "Gasthaus Königsee" with her husband Ron, in Germany.

 

·         1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour

·         1 1/2 cups ground almonds

·         a pinch of salt

·         2/3 cups sugar

·         2 sticks butter

·         2 egg yolks

·         1 tsp. vanilla extract

·         1 cup sugar, mixed with1 package vanilla sugar, for coating

 

Mix together flour, almonds, salt and sugar.  Add butter with a pastry cutter until well combined.  Add egg yolks and vanilla and knead until the dough is smooth.  Let the dough cool for a while.

 

Preheat the oven to 360° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Pinch off small balls of dough, roll them into little sausage shapes, and bend them into crescents. Bake 12 to 15 minutes.

 

While still warm, roll the crescents in the sugar / vanilla sugar mix.  If you run out of sugar, mix up some more.
