Spinach with Pine Nuts and Raisins


Delicate spinach can be used in many side dishes, but we bring you Spanish version and hope you will like it as much as we did.

Serves: 6

Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2.5lb. fresh spinach, trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 3 tablespoons raisins
  • ½ cup water
  • 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • Salt, to taste


Directions:

1. Place the spinach in a large pot. Season with salt and cover with water. Cook until spinach is just wilted.

2. Drain in a colander and squeeze the liquid with hands.

3. Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes. Stir in the raisins and cook for 3 minutes more. Stir in the pine nuts and cook for 2-3 minutes.

4. Add the spinach and cook until the water evaporates. Season with salt and pepper.

5. Serve in a bowl and drizzle with olive oil.
