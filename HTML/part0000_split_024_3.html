Silver Lemon Moons and Stars

 

A favorite of Jim and Kate's family and very pretty to look at.

 

For the cookies:

·         4 cups flour

·         1 1/2 cups sugar

·         2 cups finely grated hazelnuts

·         3 tsps. baking powder

·         3 sticks butter

·         2 eggs, lightly beaten

·         3 tsps. vanilla

 

For the glaze:

·         2 cups powdered sugar

·         2 Tbsps. melted butter

·         2 Tbsps. lemon juice

 

Mix together the flour, sugar, hazelnuts and baking powder for the cookies.  Cut in the butter until it resembles crumbs.  Combine the eggs and vanilla, and add it to the flour mixture.  Work the dough until it is smooth.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Roll out dough and cut moon and star shapes.  Bake until golden, about 6 to 8 minutes.

 

For the glaze, combine all ingredients.  If the glaze is too thick, stir in more lemon juice until the desired consistency is reached.  Optionally, decorate with little silver sparkles before the glaze sets.
