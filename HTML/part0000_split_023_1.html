Pronunciation note: Remember, ê is pronounced like eh.

Cultural note: Children in France are expected to be well mannered at a very young age. Parents monitor their children closely to make sure they know how to behave at mealtimes, in public and with adults.

Idea! Expand on your “Maman Dit” game to include parts of the body.
