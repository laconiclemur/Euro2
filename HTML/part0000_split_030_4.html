Churros


Classic Spanish treat that can be served with dark or white chocolate sauce. Whatever you choose it will be a winner.

Serves: 4

Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup caster sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil


Directions:

1. Mix the flour, sugar (reserve 1 teaspoon) and baking powder.

2. In a sauce pan bring water and olive oil to a boil.

3. Pour the water mix into the flour and stir until smooth.

4. Transfer the dough into a piping bag with a wide star-shaped nozzle.

5. Heat 2-inches of vegetable oil in a saucepan.

6. Pipe out the churros, around 3-inches and cut using kitchen scissors.

7. Fry for 5 minutes and place onto a plate. Combine the cinnamon and sugar and sprinkle the churros with the prepared mix.

8. Serve with dark chocolate.
