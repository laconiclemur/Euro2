Vocabulary:                           

lundi - (Monday)

loohn-dee

mardi - (Tuesday)

mahr-dee

mercredi - (Wednesday)

meh-kruh-dee

jeudi - (Thursday)

jhoo-dee

vendredi - (Friday)

vahnd-ruh-dee

samedi - (Saturday)

sah-muh-dee

dimanche - (Sunday)

dee-mahnsh

 

Grammar note: The days of the week in French are not capitalized as they are in English.

Example:               Aujourd’hui c’est lundi. (Today is Monday.)

Cultural note: Idea! Write the days of the week on index cards and put them all in an envelope. Let your children take turns taking out the correct day of the week and hanging it on the fridge with a magnet.
