Kate's Marbled Almond Rocha

 

You need a candy thermometer for this recipe.

 

·         1/2 cup slivered almonds

·         2 sticks butter

·         1 cup raw sugar, or Turbinado or brown cane sugar

·         3 Tbsps. boiling water

·         2 Tbsps. light corn syrup

·         1/2 cup small semi-sweet chocolate chips

·         3/4 cup chopped walnuts

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Toast the 1/2 cup almonds on a large cookie sheet for 15 minutes or until brown, stirring occasionally and watching carefully so they don't burn.  Spread the almonds out and let cool.

 

Melt the butter and sugar in a pan.  Bring to a boil and let simmer carefully for 5 minutes.  In a separate bowl, combine the corn syrup and the boiling water.  Add the syrup mixture to the sugar mixture and boil until it reaches 300° F on a candy thermometer.

 

Working quickly, pour the syrup mix over the almonds, using a spatula to smooth it into a thin layer.  Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the hot syrup layer and use the spatula to spread the chocolate chips.  Add the walnuts on top.

 

When cold break into pieces.
