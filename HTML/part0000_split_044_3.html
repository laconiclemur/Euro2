Cheese Snack Cookies

 

These unusual snack cookies predate commercially produced snacks.  They were often baked when friends came over for a bottle of wine or the men were playing cards.

 

·         1 cup flour

·         1 cup shredded hard cheese, like Parmesan or Swiss

·         6 Tbsps. butter

·         1 whole egg

·         1 egg yolk mixed with a little water, for brushing the tops

·         caraway seeds, kosher salt, poppy seeds and / or coarse pepper

 

In the food processor, mix flour and cheese.  Add the butter and process until crumbly.  Add the whole egg and work into a dough ball.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Roll out the dough.  Cut out shapes and place them on the cookie sheet.  Combine the egg yolk and water and brush the tops with the egg wash.  Sprinkle with caraway seeds, poppy seeds, kosher salt or pepper.  Bake 12 minutes.
