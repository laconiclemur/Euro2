Belgian Endive and Radish Salad

SERVES 4

PREPARATION TIME 10 minutes

2 large heads Belgian endive, halved lengthwise and leaves separated

12 radishes, thinly sliced

3 tablespoons chopped parsley leaves

1 tablespoon Chardonnay vinegar

3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

1 Toss the Belgian endive leaves, radishes and parsley together in a bowl.

2 Whisk together the Chardonnay vinegar and oil in a small bowl and season with salt and pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the leaves and toss together to combine.
