Week 13: What I Like to Do

Review: Weeks 11-12: What Do I Look Like?

Vocabulary:              

Qu’ est-ce que tu aimes faire? - (What do you like to do ?)

kays-kuh-too-ehm-fayr

J’aime lire - (I like to read.)

jhehm leer

J’aime nager - (I like to swim.)

jhehm –neh-jhay

Grammar note: Verbs are words that tell about things we can do. We add a verb to the phrase J’aime to tell what we like to do.

Cultural note: All children in France play sports. French schools are very concerned with physical fitness, la forme.

Idea! Have each child pick their own activity they like to do (not listed above). Help them look up the French verb online or in a French dictionary and make their own sentence. Suggestions: danser (to dance), chanter (to sing), jouer au football (to play soccer), jouer sur l’ordinateur (to play on the computer).
