First and foremost, start with good-quality meat. Get to know your butchers and talk to them about what you’re buying – use their knowledge and expertise. Be adventurous and try some different meats now and then, such as kid, wild boar and venison as well as the more familiar beef and lamb. All are delicious and well worth cooking. And don’t be afraid of offal. Some of the greatest of all French classics involve organ meats such as brains and sweetbreads.
