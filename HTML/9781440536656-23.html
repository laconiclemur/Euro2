Honey and Ginger-Glazed Carrots

The sweetness of the honey plays off of the spiciness of fresh ginger to make these carrots a true surprise in every mouthful!

Serves 4

Ingredients

1 cup baby carrots

2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger

1 tablespoon butter

2 teaspoons lemon juice

1 teaspoon orange zest

2 teaspoons honey

1⁄4 teaspoon pepper

1⁄4 teaspoon salt

  1. Steam the carrots until they are tender but firm, about 5 minutes.

  2. Sauté the steamed carrots with the ginger in the butter over medium-high heat for 2 minutes.

  3. Stir in the lemon juice, orange zest, honey, and pepper and reduce the heat to medium-low.

  4. Cook for a few minutes, stirring to coat the carrots with the glaze as it thickens. Season with salt and remove from heat.

  5. Divide the carrots among four small plates, or serve on one plate family style.
