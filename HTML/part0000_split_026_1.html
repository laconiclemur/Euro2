Grammar note: Remember, all French nouns are masculine or feminine. Is la salle masculine or feminine? How about la cuisine?

Cultural note: Paul Cézanne is one of the most famous painters from France. His work influenced many artists in later years. Utilize Google Images to see some of his famous works like, The Black Marble Clock.
