JEFF KOEHLER’s writing, recipes, and photographs have appeared in Saveur, Food & Wine, NPR.org, EatingWell, the Washington Post, Los Angeles Times, Christian Science Monitor, Dwell, Men’s Journal, Tin House, and Best Food Writing 2010. He is the author and photographer of Morocco: A Culinary Journey with Recipes, Rice Pasta Couscous, and La Paella, which has become the standard on Spain’s iconic dish and was named by the New York Times as a noteworthy cookbook. He moved to Spain in 1996. He lives in Barcelona with his wife and two daughters. Visit his website at www.jeff-koehler.com and follow him on Twitter @koehlercooks.
