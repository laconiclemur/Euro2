Marianne's Date Thinnies

 

One of my friends does not like chocolate!  Is this even possible?  Here are a few cookies for her.  These sweet and chewy, delicate cookies flatten out quite a bit.  Drop only small amounts onto the parchment paper.

 

·         5 oz. bag of pitted dates (1/2 of a bag)

·         3 eggs

·         1 cups sugar

·         2 packages Dr. Oetker vanilla sugar

·         2 tsps. lemon juice

·         1 1/2 cups mixed ground nuts, finely ground (hazelnuts, walnuts, almonds)

 

Slice the pitted dates in fine strips, set aside.

 

Beat together the eggs, sugar, vanilla sugar, and lemon juice with an electric mixer until very fluffy.  Fold in the nuts and dates.

 

Preheat the oven to 325° F.  Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.  Drop small mounds onto the cookie sheet.  Bake about 22 minutes.  When cool, carefully lift the cookies with a thin spatula.
