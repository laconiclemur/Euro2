Prick each sausage in 2 or 3 places with a pointed knife. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet, add the sausages, and turn them to brown them all over. Pour out the excess fat, and add enough red or white wine, cava, or hard cider to at least half-cover the sausages, and cook over medium-high heat, turning them occasionally, for 20 to 25 minutes, until they are cooked through and the liquid has almost disappeared.
