Fruitcake Cookies

 

Joyce asks her husband Dick every year if he would prefer a fruit cake or these cookies.  The cookies always win hands down.  Joyce also says, any combination of candied or dried fruit works just fine.

 

·         8 oz. pitted and chopped dates

·         4 oz. each mixed candied fruit, red cherries and pineapple

·         2 Tbsps. brandy or orange juice

·         1 cup sifted flour

·         1/4 tsp. baking soda

·         1/4 tsp. salt

·         1/2 tsp. cinnamon

·         1/2 stick butter

·         1/2 cup sugar

·         1 egg

·         1/2 cup each chopped almonds and chopped walnuts

 

Two hours before baking, soak the dried fruit in brandy or orange juice.

 

Sift the dry ingredients and set aside.

 

Cream butter and sugar.  Mix in the egg and brandy, but not the fruit.  Stir in the flour and slowly add the fruit and nuts.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Drop by teaspoon 1 inch apart on the cookie sheets.  Bake for 12 minutes.
