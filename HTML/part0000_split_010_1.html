Pronunciation note:. Sometimes in French, the letters are written but remain silent. Remember to drop the final consonant in  salut.

Cultural Note: The capital of France is Paris, but there are 22 regions within the country. To travel within the country, people can take high speed trains called TGV’s, trains à grande vitesse.

Idea! In France, it is very important for children to learn to greet others with “bonjour” and tell them “au revoir” as they leave. Adopt this French nicety by teaching your kids to not only say “please” and “thank you”, but “hello” and “goodbye” to adults they encounter. Saying it in French is bonus!
