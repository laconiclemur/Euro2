Roasted Pimento Squash


This side dish uses traditional Spain ingredients like the Manchego and pimiento and fits perfectly with meat based dishes.

Serves: 4

Time: 45 minutes + inactive time

Ingredients:

  • 1.5lb. summer squash, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 cup grated Manchego (sheep cheese)
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 ½ teaspoons hot pimenton
  • 1 teaspoon salt


Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 450F.

2. Toss the squash with salt in a colander. Place aside for 30 minutes.

3. After 30 minutes, transfer the squash to a bowl. Add the onion, pimenton, and olive oil.

4. Arrange on a baking sheet and roast for 20 minutes.

5. Flip and roast for 20 minutes more. Serve after, sprinkled with Manchego.
