Coconut Macaroons

 

If you like coconut, here is a simple recipe from my sister-in-law, Diane.

 

·         4 egg whites

·         1/2 tsp. cream of tartar

·         1 cup sugar

·         1/4 tsp. cinnamon

·         6 drops almond extract

·         7 oz. sweetened coconut flakes

 

Beat the egg whites and cream of tartar until peaks form.  Add the sugar slowly, then cinnamon and almond extract .  Fold in the coconut flakes.

 

Preheat the oven to 275° F. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Drop cookie mixture by the teaspoonful onto the cookie sheet.  Bake 35 minutes.
