Coulis de tomates

Tomato purée

2 tbsp olive oil

2 shallots, peeled and finely chopped

3 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped

1 tbsp tomato paste

1 tbsp sugar

800g tomatoes, peeled, deseeded and chopped

1 bouquet garni see here)

2 tbsp water

salt

black pepper

Heat the olive oil in a pan and sweat the shallots and garlic until soft but not coloured. Add the tomato paste and cook for another 3 minutes, then add the sugar, chopped tomatoes, bouquet garni, water and seasoning. Bring to a simmer, cover with a piece of greaseproof paper and cook for 20 minutes. Tip everything into a food processor and blend until smooth. Lovely served with spinach soufflés (see here).

Any leftover coulis can be kept in the fridge in an airtight container for up to a week and used on pizzas and pasta.
