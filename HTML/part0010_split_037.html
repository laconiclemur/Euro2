Coulis de mûres

blackberry purée

300g blackberries

60–80g icing sugar

1 tbsp clear honey

A berry coulis makes a ideal partner for ice cream or fruit desserts, such as the pear charlotte on page 283, and is very easy to make. The honey adds depth of flavour and consistency to the purée.

Blitz the blackberries with the sugar and honey in a food processor. The quantity of sugar needed depends on the ripeness of the fruit, so add a little and taste, before adding the full amount.
