To Gisele and Emily – food tastes better when I am with you

Thank you all

The making of this book would not have been possible without the professionalism of chef Francesco Dibenedetto, who worked with me and prepared the food for the photographs. I would also like to thank Noemi Guy for her skill at reading my scribbled lines on sauce-stained scrap paper; Cristian Barnett for capturing the taste of the food so perfectly on film; Miranda Harvey for her skilful eye, tips on how to relax a salad and making carrots disappear; Holly Clifton-Brown for the gorgeous illustrations; Polly Webb-Wilson for the beautiful choice of plates and her navigational skills; Jinny Johnson for being as passionate about good food as I am and for making The French Kitchen delicious to read; Amanda Harris and Lucie Stericker at Weidenfeld & Nicolson for directing operations with such care and good humour; Gérard – my brother in law – for shooting straight and filling the freezer with game; and my neighbour Annie for the ‘mucky eggs’.
