Vanilla Kipferln (Vanilla Crescents)

 

There are many versions of vanilla crescents in Germany and Austria.  Most of them call for ground hazelnuts or almonds, but not this one.  This recipe comes from Grandma Angela Miess who was born around 1890 in the Caucasus and lived  in Vienna until her death in 1972.

 

·         1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour

·         1/3 cup sugar

·         1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter

·         About 2 cups powdered sugar mixed with 2 to 3 bags of vanilla sugar

 

Combine flour and sugar.  Cut in the butter and knead the dough until smooth.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Roll dough ball into a thin "sausage" and cut off pinkie size pieces.  Shape the pieces into small crescents and place on the cookie sheet.  Bake 6 to 8 minutes.

 

These cookies are very fragile.  Let them cool about 5 minutes until they are still warm, but can be lifted without damage.  Then, roll them carefully in the powdered sugar mix.
