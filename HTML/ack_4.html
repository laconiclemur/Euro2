My mother, Larisa Frumkin, has taught me that food is a pleasure meant to be shared with others, even if my dinner parties are still nothing compared to hers. Her love is what calls me back home from Spain. My final gracias are to Barry Yourgrau, who shared in every stage of this project, from savoring the alchemical cooking of Ferran Adrià to washing the dishes after long, happy nights recipe-testing at our table.
