Nonnenfürtzle

 

Light and airy as the wind of a nun.  The German version comes from Marianne.

 

·         8 oz. slivered almonds

·         4 egg whites

·         1 3/4 cups sugar

 

Carefully toast the slivered almonds in a pan or the oven until they are light brown.

 

Preheat the oven to 300° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Beat the egg whites until stiff.  Fold in the almonds and sugar.  Drop small mounds of the meringue onto the cookie sheet.  Bake 25 minutes until lightly brown and firm in the middle.
