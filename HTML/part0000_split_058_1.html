Grammar note: If you want to say the, you use le or la.

If you want to say a, you use un or une.

Example:             

C’est une fleur. = It is a flower.

C’est la fleur. = It is the flower.

Cultural note: France is sometimes referred to as L’Hexagone because is shaped like a hexagon!

Idea! Use the question, Qu’est-ce que c’est? to practice mixing up you articles and pronouns. Try different ways of answering the same question:

It is my car/ It is the car. C’est ma voiture./ C’est la voiture.

It is a train/It is the train. C’est un train./C’est le train.
