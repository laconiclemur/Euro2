Lemon Cheesecake


Light, creamy and delicious with a hint of freshness coming from the lemon. Just like a dessert needs to be.

Serves: 10 persons

Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1.75lb. farmers cheese, beaten
  • 7 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest


Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 400F.

2. Mash the cheese with a fork.

3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar, and butter.

4. Gradually fold in the cheese, whisking well after each addition.

5. Add the cheese, lemon zest and cinnamon. Stir until just combined.

6. Transfer the batter into prepared cake pan and bake for 20 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean.

7. Transfer to a wire racks to cool. Once cooled slice and serve.
