Walnut Balls

 

This recipe comes from Tante Gretel, the older sister of my grandmother Angela.  No baking required for these sweet walnut balls

 

·         1 1/3 cups ground walnuts

·         1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

·         1 egg white

·         a little rum, to taste

 

Mix everything well in a medium bowl.  With your hands, form cherry-sized balls and place them on parchment paper.  It gets a little messy, and you might need to scrape your hands in between.

 

Let the walnut balls air dry completely or keep them in a warm oven until they can be removed from the parchment without breaking.
