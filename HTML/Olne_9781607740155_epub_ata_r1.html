About the Author

Richard Olney was born and raised in Iowa, one of eight children. After moving to a Parisian suburb in 1951 and then buying a run-down property in Provence, Olney settled in France permanently. Chief consultant to the Time-Life Good Cook series, Olney was author of eight books, including Simple French Cooking and Lulu’s Provençal Table. He passed away at his Provençal home in 1999.
