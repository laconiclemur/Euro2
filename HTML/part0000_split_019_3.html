Praline Cookie Bars

 

Very simple, but good and oh, so rich, says Irene.

 

·         1/2 cup raisins, plumped with 2 Tbsp. rum in the microwave

·         enough graham crackers to line a 10 x 15 cookie sheet

·         2 sticks butter

·         1 cup light brown sugar

·         1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

·         6 plain milk chocolate Hershey bars

 

Place the raisins in a bowl with rum in the microwave until all the liquid has been absorbed.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line the bottom of a 10 x 15 cookie sheet with graham crackers.

 

Melt the butter in a medium pan.  Stir in the brown sugar and simmer for 2 minutes.  Add the nuts and raisins.  Pour the mixture over the crackers.

 

Bake for 10 minutes.  Turn off the oven.  Remove the cookie sheet and lay the chocolate bars on top.  Return the pan to the oven just long enough to soften the chocolate.  When it has melted a bit, spread the chocolate smoothly over the entire pan.  Let cool completely and cut into bars.
