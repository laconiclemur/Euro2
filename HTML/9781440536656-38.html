Vegetable Tempura

Include this dish when you have a Japanese-themed dinner party of small plates. Tempura need to be served immediately after it’s fried, so be ready to get them from the pot to the table fast!

Serves 4

Ingredients

3⁄4 cup beer

3⁄4 cup flour

3⁄4 teaspoon salt

2 cups vegetable oil

12 stalks asparagus

12 carrot sticks

4 green onions, trimmed

  1. Whisk beer into flour until smooth, then stir in salt.

  2. Heat oil to 375°F in a deep fryer or large pot.

  3. Dip vegetables individually in batter, let excess drip off, then drop carefully into the hot oil.

  4. Cook about 3 minutes, then remove from oil with a slotted spoon, chopsticks, or tongs. Cook in batches.

  5. Drain on paper towels and serve immediately.

Deep Fry Your Candy?

Tempura batter is a good thing to dip very cold (but not frozen) candy bars in to try your hand at the deep-fried candy-bar craze so popular at British fish and chips stands.
