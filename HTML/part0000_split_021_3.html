Magic Cookie Bars

 

This old-fashioned, easy-to-do cookie bar is from Eagle Brand sweetened condensed  milk and has been a favorite in the Price family for a very long time.

 

·         1 stick butter, melted

·         1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs

·         2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

·         1 cup chopped nuts

·         1 1/3 cups coconut flakes

·         1 (14 oz.) can sweetened condensed milk

 

Preheat the oven the 350° F.  Spray a 13 x 9 cookie sheet with a non-stick spray.

 

Combine the melted butter and the graham cracker crumbs.  Press mixture into cookie sheet.  Sprinkle chocolate chips and nuts all over.  Top with the coconut flakes.  Pour the condensed milk over everything.

 

Bake 25 minutes until lightly brown.  Cool at least 15 minutes then cut into bars.
