CONFITURE D’ABRICOTS A LA VANILLE

Apricot and Vanilla Jam

 
1kg (2lb 4oz) apricots (stoned weight), about 1.3kg (3lb) before stoning
1kg (2lb 4oz) preserving sugar
Finely grated zest of 1 lemon and juice of 2
1 large moist vanilla pod

Stone and chop the apricots and mix with the sugar, lemon zest and lemon juice. Halve and scrape the vanilla pod and mix both the seeds and the pod with the apricots and sugar. Proceed as here. Pour into a bowl, stir and bottle.
