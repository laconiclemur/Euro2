(Eggplant Parmesan)

...Sitting across from Johnny over gooey Eggplant Parmigiana, Annie was as heartbroken as she appeared nonchalant.

Johnny poured the wine into her glass and handed it to her, waited for her to take a sip.

“So?” he said, a half smile on his lips, “you want to get married?”

She swallowed the wine and coughed, “Do you mean in general? I guess one day, with the right man, at the right time.”

“No, not in general. I mean the two of us. This week.”
