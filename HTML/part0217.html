MAYONNAISE

Using a whole egg gives more body to the mayonnaise.

MAKES AROUND 500ML

1 egg

2 egg yolks

juice of ½ a lemon

250ml extra virgin olive oil

250ml pomace or light olive oil

sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Whisk together the egg and yolks, then whisk in the lemon juice. Slowly, bit by bit, add the olive oil, continuing to whisk. When the mixture starts to emulsify you can add the oil a little faster. Season to taste at the end.
