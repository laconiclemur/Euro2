Kate's Black and Whites

 

A crisp little shortbread cookie dipped in chocolate and nuts.

 

For the cookies:

·         3 sticks butter, softened

·         3/4 cup sugar

·         3 cups flour

·         1 tsp. salt

·         1 tsp. vanilla

 

For dipping the cookies:

·         1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

·         1 Tbsp. shortening

·         1 1/2 cups finely chopped nuts, any variety

 

Cream butter and sugar together.  Mix in flour, salt and vanilla.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Shape the dough into 1-inch balls.  Dip the bottom of a glass into sugar and use it to flatten the balls.  Set them on parchment paper lined cookie sheets.  Bake for 10 minutes.

 

Melt the chocolate with the shortening.  Dip each cookie into the glaze until is half-covered, and then dip it into the chopped nuts.  Cool on wax paper completely before storing.
