Savory Peach Bellini

This elegant drink deserves to be surrounded by elegant small plates, so save this for a special brunch or cocktail party. Make sure the purée and wine is chilled before you assemble the drink.

Serves 1

Ingredients

1 ounce white peach purée, chilled

4 ounces Prosecco Italian sparkling wine, chilled

1 strawberry

  1. Pour the peach purée into a champagne flute.

  2. Add the sparkling wine to the flute.

  3. Garnish with a strawberry and serve.
