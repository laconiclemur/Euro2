Vinaigrette à la tomate

Tomato dressing

Makes ABOUT 500ml

4 ripe tomatoes

150ml extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for cooking the tomatoes

2 free-range egg yolks

1 tsp Dijon mustard

1½ tbsp tarragon vinegar

1 heaped tsp tomato purée

300ml vegetable oil

Tabasco sauce

Make a tomato ‘fondue’ – peel, deseed and chop 4 ripe tomatoes and cook them in a little olive oil until the mixture is thick and dry.

Make the vinaigrette as above, but replace the herbs with 1 heaped teaspoon of tomato purée and 1 heaped tablespoon of tomato ‘fondue’. Season with a few drops of Tabasco sauce instead of pepper.
