chicken broth with sherry and ground almonds

{ consomé al jerez y almendras–andalusia }

Serve this elegant silky soup in cups. Make the chicken stock on page 125, or buy a good-quality one.

SERVES 2

1 cup chicken stock

⅓ cup dry or medium-dry sherry

¼ cup ground almonds

Salt

3 large egg yolks

Bring the stock and sherry to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in the almonds and a little salt, taking into account the saltiness of the stock, and simmer for 2 minutes.

Just before serving, bring the soup back to a simmer. Beat the egg yolks in a small bowl, beat in a ladleful of the broth, and then pour back into the pan, stirring vigorously. Continue to stir for a few seconds over low heat; do not let the soup boil, or the yolks will curdle.
