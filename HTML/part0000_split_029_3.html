Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookies Without Flour

 

Wilhelmina immigrated in 1957 to America from the Nuremberg area.  She has baked these no-flour cookies for a very long time already and always keeps a batch in the freezer.  Take out a few at a time for a delectable coffee break.

 

·         1 stick unsalted butter

·         1 1/2 cups white sugar

·         1 1/2 cups packed brown sugar

·         4 eggs

·         1 tsp. vanilla

·         1 - 18 oz. jar chunky peanut butter

·         6 cups oats

·         2 1/2 tsps. baking soda

·         1-12 oz. chocolate chips

 

Cream together butter, both sugars, the eggs and the vanilla.  Stir in the peanut butter until smooth.  Add the oats, the baking soda and the chocolate chips.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.  Drop the dough by walnut size balls onto the cookie sheets. Bake 10 minutes.

 

Andy says - These cookies are very yummy.  Omit the chocolate chips and substitute  2 cups chopped dried apples.
