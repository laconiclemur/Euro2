sherry shallot vinaigrette

vinaigrette allo scalogno

This dressing is appropriate for any simple green salad, and may also be used on the Arugula with Prosciutto, Pears, and Parmesan salad. It may be stored for up to two weeks in the refrigerator. However, the shallots should be removed after one day; this may be done by straining the dressing through a fine-mesh sieve. Allow the dressing to return to room temperature before whisking briskly and serving over tossed greens.

1 small shallot, finely diced

1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

1/4 cup sherry vinegar

1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

1/4 cup canola oil

3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon hot water

In a small bowl or wide-mouthed jar, whisk the shallot, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Slowly add the canola oil, olive oil, and the hot water, whisking to make a smooth, emulsified dressing. Set aside until needed.

—MAKES 1 CUP —
