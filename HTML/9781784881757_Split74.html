CROSTINI AL SUGO DI POMODORO E VONGOLE

toasted bread with tomato & clam sauce

This is a good way to use up the tomato and clam sauce on cheat’s spaghetti with clams. It makes a delicious seafood-flavour canapé and goes down very well with a glass of Prosecco.

SERVES 8 PEOPLE

½ quantity of tomato and clam sauce (see cheat’s spaghetti with clams)

TO SERVE

16 small slices of bread from a baguette

small handful of parsley, roughly chopped

Put the clam sauce in a saucepan over a medium heat and let it reduce until it is dense and more like a spread than a sauce – around 20 minutes should do it. To serve, toast the bread as in the recipe for Toasted Bread Topped with Chicken Liver Pâté. Top the bread with the sauce (there is no need to dip it in the stock this time). Garnish each one with a little parsley and serve straight away.
