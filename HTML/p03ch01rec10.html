to prepare small artichoke hearts

With a small sharp knife, cut off the stem of each artichoke. Cut away or pull off the tough outer leaves until you are left with the pale inner leaves. Slice off the tough ends of these, then open them with your fingers and scoop out the prickly inner choke with a pointed spoon. Drop the artichokes into water acidulated with lemon juice or vinegar to prevent the exposed parts from darkening.
