Week 5: How Old Are You?

Review: Week 4: What is Your Name?

Vocabulary:                           

Quel âge as-tu? - (How old are you?)

kehl-hajh-ah-too

J’ai______ans. - (I am ____ years old.)

jhay_____ahn

Pronunciation note: The letter “j” in French produces a sound somewhere between an English j, a z and sh. Throughout this book, it is referred to as jh within the pronunciation guide. The best thing you can do is listen to spoken French the proper sound.

Cultural Note: Have you ever heard of Champagne? Well it is not just a drink, it is the name of the region in which it is made! Other popular wine growing regions are Burgundy and Bordeaux

Idea! Teach your child the French number for their age. Then interview each other as if you just met using the vocabulary from the previous lessons. Siblings can interview each other!
