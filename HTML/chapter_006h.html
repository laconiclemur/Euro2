VINAIGRETTE A LA MOUTARDE

Dijon Mustard Vinaigrette

This is a very creamy vinaigrette, which is as good with leaves as it is with leeks or finely grated beetroot or carrot. Makes a punchy salad to accompany a roast chicken or joint.

MAKES 250ml (9fl oz)
1 tsp fine salt
Â½ tsp caster sugar
2 tbsp each of white wine vinegar and water
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
200ml (7fl oz) vegetable oil

Mix together the salt, sugar, white wine vinegar and water, and stir to dissolve the salt and sugar. Add the mustard, and finally whisk in the oil. Store in a screw-top container, and whisk or shake well each time you use it.
