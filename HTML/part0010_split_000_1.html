Stocks are the cornerstones of great cooking. You can buy good fresh stocks now, of course, but there’s nothing like making your own for the best results. They’re easy to prepare and allow you to make use of all the bones and trimmings from your meat and fish so there’s no waste! I’m a big fan of sauces too, which can provide the finishing touch to a dish. Every keen cook should try to learn the basics, like a good meat jus and a classic vinaigrette. You’ll also find some other little extras in this chapter, which can help to make your cooking something special.
