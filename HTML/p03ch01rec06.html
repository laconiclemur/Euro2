to clean and prepare squid

Pull the head of each squid away from the body and discard the soft innards that come out with it. Discard the insides of the body: the ink sac, if any, the icicle-shaped translucent bone, and any soft innards. Leave the tentacles in their bunches, but remove the eyes and the small round beak (cartilage) at the base of the tentacles by cutting them away with a sharp knife just below the eyes (be careful so that ink doesnâ€™t squirt out at you from the eyes). Rinse thoroughly.
