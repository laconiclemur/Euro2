Ingrid's Date Balls

 

These are a labor of love, but worth it.  Stirring the ingredients alone takes work.

 

·         1 stick butter

·         1/2 tsp. salt

·         1 cup light brown sugar

·         1 1/2 cups pitted dates, chopped

·         1 cup chopped pecans

·         1 cup coconut flakes

·         2 cups Rice Crispy cereal

 

Melt the butter with the salt and brown sugar.  Let it bubble for 3 minutes, stirring constantly.  Add the dates, pecans, coconut flakes and rice cereal.

 

Using your hands, shape into walnut-sized balls.  Roll the balls in powdered sugar.  Be careful not to burn your hands!  The mass will be VERY HOT when shaping the balls.

 

As it cools, it does not stick well anymore, and the last cup or two may not like to stick at all.  Keep the leftovers and sprinkle it over yogurt or ice cream - a yummy yummy topping.
