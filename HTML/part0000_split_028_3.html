Peanut Butter Bars

 

Barbara e-mailed this sweet and salty bar cookie.  The dough is quite stiff and best prepared in a standing mixer while scraping down the sides frequently.

 

For the bars:

·         2 cups flour

·         2 tsps. baking powder

·         1 tsp. baking soda

·         1/2 tsp. salt

·         1/2 cup shortening

·         1/2 cup white sugar

·         1/2 cup packed brown sugar

·         2 egg yolks

·         1 tsp. vanilla

·         3 Tbsps. cold water

·         1 - 12 oz. package semi-sweet chocolate chips

 

For the topping:

·         2 egg whites

·         1 cup packed brown sugar

·         3/4 cup salted peanuts, do not use dry roasted nuts

 

Sift together the flour, baking powder and baking soda.  Set aside.

 

Cream the shortening.  Slowly add both sugars, the egg yolks and vanilla.  Work in the dry ingredients, alternating with the cold water.

 

Preheat the oven to 325° F.  Spray a 13 X 9 pan with cooking spray.  Press the dough into the pan and top with the chocolate chips.

 

Whisk the egg whites until foam.  Slowly add the brown sugar and beat until stiff.  Spread over the chocolate chips and top with the salted peanuts.

 

Bake 30 to 35 minutes.  Cut into bars while still warm.
