Mantecados


Spanish shortbread cookies similar to polvorones. They contain almond flour and are drizzled with chocolate; simply great for cold winter days.

Serves: 24 cookies

Time: 15 minutes + inactive time

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 12oz. chocolate chips
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup chopped blanched almonds
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest


Directions:

1. Cream the butter and sugar in a bowl.

2. Add the almonds, lemon zest and flour. Mix gently until ingredients are fully incorporated.

3. Divide the dough into three equal portions. Wrap in a plastic foil and chill for 1 hour.

4. Roll out the chilled dough and cut out the cookies using a cookie cutter.

5. Transfer the cutout cookie onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper.

6. Whisk the egg and 2 teaspoons water in a bowl. Brush the cookies with egg wash before baking.

7. Bake for 10 minutes at 370F.

8. Melt the chocolate chips in a microwave. You can either serve the cookies with chocolate sauce or drizzle with the chocolate before serving.
