(Fruit Tartlets)

...Annie and the children walked back home and she listened to their day and let them talk her into a stop at the pâtisserie. The strawberry tartelettes were ridiculously expensive with strawberries being so entirely out of season, but she said yes.

“You said it was too expensive yesterday,” Maxence pointed out.

“It’s cheaper today.”

“It’s the same price. Look: 2.5 Euros each.”

Annie sighed. “I’ll have one too after all,” she told the boulangère.
