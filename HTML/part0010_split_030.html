Chips de salsifis

Salsify crisps

1 salsify

oil, for deep-frying

Peel the salsify and slice lengthways on a mandolin, making the slices as thin as possible. Heat the oil to 180Â°C in a large pan or a deep-fat fryer. Add the slices of salsify, a few at a time, and deep-fry until crisp. Drain them on kitchen paper to absorb any excess fat, then serve.

This method can be used to make chips from any root vegetables.
