Roasted Carrots – Spanish Style


Crisp-tender carrots with a hint of fresh lime juice are perfect side dish for many dinner or lunch ideas.

Serves: 6

Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3lb. carrots, peeled and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup roasted, shelled and chopped pistachios
  • 1 tablespoon chopped mint leaves
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 4 drops hot sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • ½ cup chopped parsley
  • Salt, to taste


Directions:

1. Process half the pistachios in a food blender until quite fine.

2. Add the mint and parsley and process until finely chopped. Add the cheese, butter, hot sauce and season with salt. Scrape the butter mix and transfer onto a plastic foil. Wrap and shape into a log. Freeze until firm.

3. Once firm remove from the freezer. In a sauce pan combine the carrots and some salt. Add enough water just to cover. Bring to boil and reduce heat after. Simmer for 5 minutes or until crisp-tender. Drain the carrots, reserving ¼ cup cooking water.

4. Return the carrots back to the pan and add in the prepared butter. Add the lime juice and reserved cooking liquid. Season to taste and sprinkle with reserved chopped pistachios.
