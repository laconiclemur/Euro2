Turron - Sweets

 

Peter and Helga lived in South Africa where the seasons are reversed.  Christmas came at the hottest time of the year, so Helga loved to make these no-bake, sweet, little treats which she received from a Spanish neighbor.  There, the logs were dried in the sun, but here we best use a warm oven.  These "candies" are similar to marzipan confection.

 

·         3 cups finely ground hazelnuts

·         1 cup sugar

·         1 cup diced candied fruit

·         2 packages Dr. Oetker Vanilla sugar

·         1 oz. rum or rum extract

 

In a pan, mix all ingredients.  Over low heat, stir mixture continuously until firm.  If necessary, add more rum or use water by the teaspoon until everything sticks together.

 

While still warm, shape it into several small logs with square side, or roll the mixture into small balls instead, which is easier.  Place logs or balls on a cookie sheet and let dry in a warm oven.  When cold, cut into small pieces.
