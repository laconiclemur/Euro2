about the authors

Owners of London’s Caffè Caldesi, Caldesi in Campagna in Bray, and the Marylebone La Cucina Caldesi cooking school, Katie and Giancarlo Caldesi have a passion for Italian food. They have spent over 16 years teaching students at every level, and have written 10 cookbooks. Katie and Giancarlo have two children, Giorgio and Flavio.
