Marzipan Potatoes

 

Form small balls of Marzipan, either purchased or from your own production.  Shape the balls into potatoes and put them into a sieve.  Sift cocoa powder over them and shake gently to cover all the potatoes.  With a dull knife, make 3 small cuts so they resemble the popped skin of a potato.  The cocoa cuts down on the sweetness of the Marzipan.
