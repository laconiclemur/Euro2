LISA ABEND is a journalist based in Madrid. Since 2005, she has been Time magazine’s correspondent in Spain. She also contributes to a number of newspapers and magazines, including The Atlantic, The New York Times, Food & Wine, The Nation, Saveur, Wired, and Slate. In a previous life (that is, about six years ago) she was a professor of Spanish history at Oberlin College.
