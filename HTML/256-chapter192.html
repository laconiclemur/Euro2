CUTTING A RABBIT INTO 8 OR 9 PIECES

Open the cavity and pull out the organs, reserving the liver if desired. Cut away the hind legs and separate them from the loin, and then do the same with the forelegs. Cut the loin crosswise into two or three pieces. Working between rib bones, cut the ribcage crosswise into two pieces. There should be eight or nine serving pieces.
