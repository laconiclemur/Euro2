Week 32: Review

  • Learning all this useful information requires a lot of memorization.
    • Use playing cards or preschool counting flashcards to review numbers.
    • Count cars on the road. How many…trucks, red cars, motorcycles, etc.
    • Practice your days of the week song or create your own.
    • Try some fun games and videos available online. I like the games on digitaldialects.com/french
    • and bbc.co.uk/schools/primarylanguages/french
    • Have a group birthday party. Bake profiteroles or another tasty French treat. Everyone has to state their birthday in order to get a taste.
  • Learn more about the food of France
    • Try feeding your children an afternoon goûter.
    • Visit a French bakery.
    • Structure a dinner the French way, course by course.
    • Enjoy a croissant for breakfast.
    • Introduce your child to some vegetables that are seldom used in North America, like endive, fennel or celery root.
