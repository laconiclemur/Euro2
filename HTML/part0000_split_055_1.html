Review: Week 46: It’s Time to Go!

Vocabulary:

Bonne journée! - (Have a nice day!)

buhn jhoor-nay

Pronunciaton note: Bonne (buhn) is pronounced differently than Bon (bahn).

Cultural note: New Orleans is a city in Louisiana that retains much of its French culture. Some residents speak Cajun French or Creole French, different types of North American French.

Idea! Find a French restaurant and get lunch. Make sure to  wish the waiter, “ Bonne journée!”.
