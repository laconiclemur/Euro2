Dijon Stone-Crab Claws

If you can’t locate stone crabs in your local fish market, there are several online sources that can ship them to you. This simple dish relies almost entirely on the unique and decadent flavor of these special crabs.

Serves 4

Ingredients

8 cooked stone-crab claws, thawed

1⁄2 cup mayonnaise

3 tablespoons Dijon mustard

  1. To thaw the frozen stone-crab claws, place in the refrigerator for 12 to 18 hours.

  2. Combine the mayonnaise and Dijon mustard.

  3. Crack the claws with a mallet and serve chilled with the mayonnaise mustard sauce for dipping.
