VINAIGRETTE BALSAMIQUE

Balsamic Vinaigrette

A simple salad of leaves can become sublime through the addition of a well-made vinaigrette. As much care should be taken in shaking the vinaigrette as in making it, to ensure all the ingredients are well combined. Here the two vinegars wonderfully balance sweet and sour. Or just use this basic recipe as a starting point for exploration: you can add herbs such as basil and mint, or some finely diced red onions and, if you’re among friends, garlic.

MAKES 250ml (9fl oz)
1 tsp each of salt and caster sugar
½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
3 tbsp each of balsamic vinegar and water
½ tbsp white or red wine vinegar
200ml (7fl oz) olive oil

Mix together the salt, sugar, pepper, vinegars and water, and stir to dissolve the salt and sugar. Add the olive oil and whisk in. Store in a screw-top jar, and whisk or shake well each time you use it.
