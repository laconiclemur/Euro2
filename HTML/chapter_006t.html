CONFITURE AUX FIGUES ET MURES

Fig and Blackberry Jam

 
600g (1lb 5oz) purple figs
400g (14oz) blackberries
1kg (2lb 4oz) preserving sugar
Roughly grated zest and juice of 1 lemon

Chop each fig into about six, then toss together with the blackberries, sugar, lemon zest and juice. Don’t break up the blackberries. Allow the sugar to dissolve completely, then gently transfer the lot to a saucepan just slightly too large for the amount you have.

Gently bring the fruit mixture to the boil and boil for 6 minutes only, stirring occasionally and skimming off any scum that rises. Pour into a bowl and stir gently every now and then as it cools to distribute the fruit evenly. Bottle just before the jam has set.
