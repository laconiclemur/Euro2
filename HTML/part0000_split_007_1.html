Words and Phrases to Get You Started

As an individual, you are embarking on a journey to learn French. As a parent, you are additionally taking on the role of teacher. With this knowledge in mind, prepare yourself from day one with some basic vocabulary that will guide and encourage your children. It also adds to the language they will pick up through exposure!

Très bien!: - Very good!

Fantastique!: - Fantastic!

Merveilleux!: - Wonderful!

Dis- le moi encore une fois: - Tell me again.

Répète, s’il te plaît: - Repeat, please.

Essaie encore: - Try again.

Sois attentif!: -               Pay attention!

Calme-toi: -               Calm down.

Attends: - Wait.

Viens ici: - Come here.

Beau travail!: -               Good job!

Écoute-moi : - Listen to me.

Dans une seconde…: - In a second….

And don’t forget……Je t’aime: - I love you!
