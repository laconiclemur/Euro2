Honey Ginger Peanuts

With a distinctly Asian flair, these peanuts taste great on their own, or as part of a cold sesame noodle salad.

Serves 4

Ingredients

2 tablespoons heavy cream

1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger

1 teaspoon soy sauce

1⁄4 cup honey

2 cups shelled peanuts, roasted but unsalted

Pinch kosher salt

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.

  2. Combine the cream, ginger, soy sauce, and honey in a bowl with a fork. Add the peanuts and stir to coat.

  3. Pour the coated peanuts onto a baking sheet lined with parchment and separate them into individual nuts with a fork.

  4. Bake for 10 minutes, stir the nuts around, and bake another 5 minutes. Sprinkle nuts with salt, and let cool on the parchment. Store in a tin with a tight-fitting lid.
