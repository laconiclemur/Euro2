Pat's Peanut Butter Cookies

 

Easy to do, pure peanut butter taste, and a little unusual.  I was skeptical that it would work because it has no flour in it or anything else to keep it together.  For best results, stir the ingredients by hand.

 

·         1 cup peanut butter, smooth or chunky

·         1 cup sugar

·         1 egg

·         1 tsp. soda

·         vanilla extract and chocolate chips, optionally

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.  Mix all ingredients together.  Drop the dough by the teaspoonful onto cookie sheets.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes until golden.
