Olive Tapenade


Classic Spanish olive tapenade is great to prepare when you are in a rush and do not want to spend more than 10 minutes on appetizer preparation.

Serves: 10

Time: 10 minutes + inactive time

Ingredients:

  • 4 oil-packed anchovy fillets
  • 4 teaspoons grappa (grape-based spirit)
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 tablespoons salt-packed capers
  • 1lb. green olives

Additional:

  • 1 baguette sliced into ½-inch thick slices


Directions:

1. Remove the backbones from anchovy.

2. Soak the fillets in a cold water for 20 minutes. Remove and pat dry.

3. Process the olives in a blender until coarsely chopped.

4. In a bowl, combine the anchovies, olives, garlic, olive oil, capers, and grappa.

5. Let it sit for 1 hour.

6. Before serving to brush the baguette slices with olive oil. Grill or toast and spread over prepared tapenade.
