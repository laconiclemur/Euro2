Spanish Flan


Creamy yet smooth, rich and milky Spanish dessert.

Serves: 6

Time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 12fl.oz. evaporated milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 14fl.oz. condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract


Directions:

1. Beat the eggs in a bowl. Add the condensed milk, evaporated milk and vanilla. Beat until blended.

2. Place the sugar in a saucepan and heat over medium heat until sugar is melted and caramelized.

3. Once the sugar has golden brown color transfer into a 9-inch baking pan. Pour over egg mixture.

4. Preheat oven to 350F. Bake the flan for 1 hour.

5. Invert and serve cold.
