Chutney de tomates aux épices

spicy tomato chutney

Makes 2 jars

600g ripe red tomatoes

½ tsp ground ginger

1 tsp chilli powder

2 tsp salt

2 tsp tomato paste

3 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped

125ml malt vinegar

120g light muscovado sugar

50g raisins

Blanch and skin the tomatoes, then cut them in half, remove the seeds and chop them roughly. Put the chopped tomatoes in a pan with the ginger, chilli, salt, tomato paste and finely chopped garlic. Simmer over a low heat until pulpy.

Add the vinegar, sugar and raisins and simmer until the mixture thickens. Stir occasionally to prevent it sticking to the bottom of the pan. Pour the chutney into sterilised glass jars, leave to cool and store in the fridge.
