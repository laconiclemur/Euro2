Fragrant Basmati Rice

A perfect accompaniment to samosas, this rice comes together easily and benefits from the addition of saffron, peas, and almonds.

Serves 4

Ingredients

13⁄4 cups water

Pinch kosher salt

1 cup basmati rice

Pinch saffron

1⁄4 cup frozen peas

2 tablespoons sliced almonds

  1. Bring the water to a boil. Add the salt and rice, stir, cover, and lower the heat to low. Set timer for 20 minutes. Do not remove the lid until then.

  2. Turn the heat off, lift the lid, add the saffron, peas, and almonds, and replace the lid.

  3. Let stand until the peas are hot, about 10 minutes. Serve immediately.
