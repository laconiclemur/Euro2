STANLEY TUCCI is an Emmy and Golden Globe Award-winning actor who has also been nominated for an Academy Award, a Tony, and a Grammy, among countless other critical and professional accolades. He has appeared in more than fifty films, including The Lovely Bones, Julie & Julia, The Devil Wears Prada, The Terminal, and Road to Perdition, as well as more than a dozen plays on and off Broadway. His acclaimed film Big Night, which he directed and starred in, is in part the inspiration for The Tucci Cookbook.

Stanley is donating a portion of his proceeds from this book to the Food Bank For New York City. To learn more about the nonprofit organization, please visit foodbanknyc.org.
