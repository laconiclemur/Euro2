FRAGOLE IN SALSA DI AGRUMI/PESCHE AL VINO ROSSO

strawberries with orange & lemon juice

Strawberries are often served with lemon juice in Italy, or a combination of lemon and orange. If you have other berries to hand, do mix them in as well. Slices of ripe nectarine make a lovely addition. If the strawberries are not sweet enough, add honey to the juices (but you may not need any).

Mix the lemon and orange juices in a bowl with the honey to taste. Arrange the strawberry slices on a large serving platter with any other berries or fruit (if using). Splash on the sweet juice and serve, or chill in the fridge for up to 3 hours. Dress with a few mint leaves just before serving.
