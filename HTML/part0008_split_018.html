Poires pochées aux amandes et chocolat

Poached pears with chocolate almond sauce

Pears and chocolate are a fabulous combination and one of my favourites. Serve these pears warm with the chocolate sauce and, if feeling particularly indulgent, some whipped cream.

Serves 4

350g caster sugar

1 vanilla pod, split and seeds scraped out

500ml water

4 pears (Williams or similar)

whipped cream (optional)

Chocolate almond sauce

250ml water

60g unsweetened cocoa powder

120g caster sugar

30g unsalted butter

40g bitter chocolate, broken into pieces

60g almonds, toasted and chopped

Put the sugar, scraped vanilla pod and seeds with the water in a pan and bring to the boil. Peel the pears and remove their cores from the base, then place them in the simmering syrup. Cover with greaseproof paper and cook until a knife pierces the pear easily – the exact time will depend on the ripeness of the pear. Leave the pears in the syrup to cool slightly while you make the sauce.

To make the sauce, boil the water with the cocoa powder and sugar, while whisking vigorously. Take the pan off the heat and whisk in the butter and chocolate, then add the almonds. Serve with the poached pears.
