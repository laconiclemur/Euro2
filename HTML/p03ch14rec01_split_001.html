DRINKS

{ bebidas }

Drinking places in Spain are tavernas and tascas (wine bars), cervecerías (beer houses), and sidreriás (cider houses). Brandy distilled from fermented grape juice is produced mainly by the sherry houses of Andalusia and in Catalonia. Strong alcoholic spirits called aguardientes, made from fermented and distilled fruit and grain musts, are produced in different parts of Spain. Many refreshing fruity alcoholic and nonalcoholic drinks and heartwarming ones are a part of Spanish tradition that is worth adopting.
