Glace au miel

honey ice cream

Serves 8

500ml milk

4 free-range egg yolks

250g flower-scented clear honey

300ml double cream

Bring the milk to the boil in a saucepan. Meanwhile, whisk the eggs with the honey until thick and creamy, then slowly whisk in the cream. Pour the boiling milk on to the yolk mixture and whisk vigorously. Strain the mixture through a sieve and leave to cool. Chill, then churn in an ice-cream machine until frozen.
