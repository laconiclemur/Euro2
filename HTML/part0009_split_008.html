Pain au cacao

Chocolate bread

This sweet, bitter bread is perfect served with rich winter dishes such as braised meats or game.

1 loaf

15g yeast

360ml tepid water

400g white bread flour

100g extra-bitter cocoa powder, plus extra for dusting

1 tbsp brown sugar

1 tsp salt

Dissolve the yeast in a little of the tepid water in a large bowl. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix until the dough comes together, adding a couple drops more water if needed. Knead for 10 minutes until the dough is supple and elastic, then cover and leave to rise for an hour.
