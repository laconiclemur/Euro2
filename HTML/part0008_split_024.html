Glace aux pruneaux et Armagnac

Prune and Armagnac ice cream

Serves 12

6 Agen prunes

2 tbsp demerara sugar

120ml Armagnac

1 quantity of vanilla ice cream (see above)

Prepare the prunes a week ahead. Remove the stones and put the prunes in a bowl, then sprinkle them with sugar and douse with the Armagnac. Cover tightly and refrigerate.
