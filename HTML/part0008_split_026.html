Glaces aux bananes et rhum

Banana and rum ice cream

Serves 12

1kg very ripe bananas (peeled weight)

650g caster sugar

175ml good-quality dark rum

juice of 1 lemon

1 litre single cream

If the bananas are not fully ripe, leave them in a warm place or even put them in a very low oven for 3 hours.

Peel the bananas and blitz them in a food processor or blender with the sugar, rum and lemon juice. Slowly add the cream, blending until smooth. Pour the mixture into an ice-cream machine and churn until frozen.
