Widow's Kisses

 

This is the Australian version of the Nonnenfürtzle and came from Thelma.

 

·         2 egg whites

·         1/4 tsp. cream of tartar

·         pinch of salt

·         1 tsp. vanilla

·         3/4 cup sugar

·         1 - 6 oz. bag of chocolate chips

·         1/4 cup chopped walnuts

 

Beat the egg whites with cream of tartar, salt and vanilla until soft peaks form.  Slowly add the sugar until the whites are stiff.  Fold in the chocolate chips and nuts.

 

Preheat the oven to 300° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Drop small mounds on the cookie sheet.  Bake for 25 minutes.
