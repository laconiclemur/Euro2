pork sausages with caramelized pears

{ botifarra amb peres–catalonia }

Prick each sausage in 2 or 3 places with a pointed knife. Fry them in a little oil in a large skillet over low heat, turning them occasionally, for 20 to 25 minutes, until they are cooked through and well browned; or cook them in wine as on page 434. Have 1 slightly unripe pear, such as Comice or Conference, per person. Peel, core, and cut into 6 slices each, and sauté in a little oil over medium heat, turning them, until they are soft and lightly browned all over. Serve with the sausages.
