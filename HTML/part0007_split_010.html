Pommes gaufrettes

Game chips

These crispy waffle-style chips (left) can be cut on a mandolin with a waffle blade. Perfect with good plain meat such as grilled fillet of horse (see here).

Serves 2–3

2–3 chipping potatoes, such as Roosters

1kg duck fat or dripping, for frying

salt

Peel the potatoes, then slice them as thinly as possible on a mandolin. Rinse in cold water, then drain and pat dry.

Heat the duck fat or dripping in a deep-fat fryer and fry the game chips a batch at a time until golden and crispy. Drain and season with a little salt.
