Beurre blanc

butter sauce

Makes about 300ml

100ml dry white wine

1 tbsp white wine vinegar

2 shallots, peeled and finely chopped

50ml double cream

200g cold unsalted butter, cubed

salt

black pepper

Put the wine, vinegar and shallots in a thick-based saucepan. Bring to the boil and continue to cook until the liquid is reduced by half. Add the cream and boil for 1 minute, then lower the heat and gradually whisk in the cubes of cold butter.

I like to keep the shallots in the sauce, but if you prefer a smoother finish, pass the sauce through a fine sieve. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
