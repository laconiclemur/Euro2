Catalan Potatoes


Catalan potatoes bring you everything a side dish needs to offer: great texture, amazing color, and outstanding aroma.

Serves: 6

Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1lb. Idaho potatoes, peeled and sliced to ¼-inch thick
  • 4 plum tomatoes, peeled, seeded and diced
  • 1 Spanish onion diced
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 garlic cloves, whole
  • Black pepper, to taste


Directions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet. Add the garlic and cook until browned.

2. Discard the garlic and add the onion. Cook for 12 minutes over medium heat. Remove from the pan with a slotted spoon.

3. Add half of the potatoes with an even layer of garlic, onion and tomatoes. Season to taste and top with remaining potato slices. Continue cooking for 15 minutes more.

4. Once done slide to the plate and serve.
