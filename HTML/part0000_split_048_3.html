Marzipan Coins with Apricots

 

Marianne says these take a little extra work but are super delicious treats.

 

·         1 pound dried apricots

·         2 Tbsps. apricot or peach Schnapps or orange juice, or more if needed

·         3/4 cup sugar

·         1 Tbsp. orange marmalade

·         1 pound Marzipan paste

·         about 2 3/4 cups powdered sugar, for kneading the dough

 

Dice the apricots and add the Schnapps.  Plump them in the microwave until all the liquid has been absorbed.

 

In the food processor, puree the apricots.  Add the sugar and orange marmalade and mix well.  Set aside.

 

With your hands, knead the Marzipan paste with enough powdered sugar until it looks very white.

 

On a board covered with powdered sugar, roll out the Marzipan about 1/2" thick into a rectangle.  Place the apricot puree on top of the Marzipan.  Starting from the long side, roll the dough up into a log.  Pinch the seams closed.  Rest the roll 1 to 2 hours in the fridge.

 

With a sharp knife, cut thick slices to look like coins.  They are ready to eat!
