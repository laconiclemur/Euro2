jhay-voo-pohr-tayr

un tee-shirt - (tee shirt)

ahn-tee-shirt

un pantalón - (pants)

ahn-pahn-tah-lohn

uhn rohb

une jupe - (skirt)

uhn jhewp

un short - (shorts)

ahn-short

Grammar note: Remember number agreement. You must match your pronoun or article to your noun.

Cultural note: Morocco is an African country located on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. The people of Morocco speak Arabic, but French remains the language of business.

Idea! Before you put your kids to bed, help them pick out their outfits using their French vocabulary.
