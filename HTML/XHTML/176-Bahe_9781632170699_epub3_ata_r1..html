MELISSA BAHEN is the author of the popular blog Lulu the Baker, where she focuses on fresh recipes and modern country life. She’s also the co-founder of The Hello Sessions, a conference for bloggers and creative entrepreneurs in the Pacific Northwest. She regularly writes for the Style Spotters blog for Better Homes & Gardens, and has had her work featured in numerous digital and print publications. She lives in the rolling hills of western Oregon with her husband, four children, eight chickens, and a beehive. This is her first book.
