Risotto con Peperoni e Melanzane

1. Eggplants are either male or female. Males have more seeds and can be identified by an oval spot at their bottom ends. The females are almost seedless and are much less bitter. Look at its bottom. A small brown round spot indentation identifies its sex as female. Choose the female. They are much sweeter.
