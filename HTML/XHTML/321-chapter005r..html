HELPFUL THINGS TO KNOW ABOUT WINE

1. Grapes are the only fruit that ferments naturally. When ambient yeast comes into contact with sugar, wine is born.

2. Vin de pays, or VDP, is a title given to “country wines.” It’s been used by savvy vignerons since 1968 as a way to use varietal classification that’s free from restrictive AOC laws.

HOW MANY GRAPES IN A GLASS?

720 bottles of wine = 1 ton of grapes

1 bottle of wine = 2.8 pounds of grapes

1 glass of wine = 0.7 pounds of grapes
