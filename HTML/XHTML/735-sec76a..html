SPANISH BLT

I’m an all-American girl at heart with an abiding love of BLTs. I didn’t think the original could get any better until I shoved a slice of tortilla in there and smeared the bread with olive oil mayo.

THIS SANDWICH IS A PRETTY BASIC ONE MADE BY toasting whole-grain sandwich bread, spreading the slices with Arbequina Olive Oil Mayonnaise (here) then sandwiching in thick slices of Tortilla Española (here) tomato slices, lettuce leaves, and crisped thick-cut bacon. That’s it: a BLT with tortilla.
