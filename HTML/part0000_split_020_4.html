Green Beans with Almonds


Spicy, crunchy and pairs wonderfully with any kind of meat.

Serves: 4

Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium shallots, thinly sliced
  • 0.75lb. green beans, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 ½ teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon smoked hot paprika
  • 1/3 cup chopped almonds


Directions:

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add the green beans and cook for 5 minutes. Drain and run under cold water.

2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet. Add the shallots and cook over medium-high heat.

3. Cook for 2 minutes. Add the sugar and cook for 45 seconds stirring constantly.

4. Add the almonds and smoked paprika; stir well.

5. Add the beans and cook for 2 minutes.

6. Season to taste before serving.
