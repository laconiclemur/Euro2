Edith's Sugar Cookies

 

Edith owned a German import gift store where, every afternoon, in the German tradition, she served coffee and sweets to her employees.

 

·         2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour

·         3/4 cups sugar

·         3 sticks unsalted butter

·         3 egg yolks, lightly beaten

·         jam, your favorite flavor

·         2 packages Dr. Oetker Vanilla sugar, mixed with 1 cup regular sugar

 

In a food processor, pulse flour and sugar to mix.  Cut in the butter until it resembles tiny crumbs.  Add the lightly beaten eggs and let it process until it forms a ball.

 

Preheat the oven to 350° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Roll out the dough and cut out cookies, using only simple forms.  Bake 6 minutes.

 

While still warm, spread the back of one cookie with your favorite jam and cover with a second cookie.  Roll the cookie in vanilla sugar.
