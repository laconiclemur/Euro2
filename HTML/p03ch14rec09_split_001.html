laced coffee

{ carajillo }

Drinking coffee laced with rum began in Cuba. Carajillo is now made in northern Spain with brandy or orujo—the fiery alcohol from the residue of wine grapes—as well as with rum. For each person, fill a small cup three-quarters full of strong black coffee, sweeten it, if you like, with sugar, and add a tablespoon or more of rum, brandy, or orujo.
