Unit 5: Mealtimes

Week 33: I’m Hungry

Review: Concepts from Weeks 25-32

Vocabulary:

Tu as faim? - (Are you hungry?)

too-ah-fahn

J’ai faim. - (I’m hungry.)

jhee-fahn

Grammar note: In French, when we answer a question in the negative, the word no is expressed as ne pas.

Example:                            Tu as faim?                            Non. Je n’ai pas faim.

A contraction is used to express the word ne. If this is too confusing, you can stick with a simple, Non.

Cultural note: Gustave Eiffel was the designer of the famous Eiffel Tower. In 1889, when it was built, it was the tallest structure in the world!

Idea! This is one of the easiest conversations to practice because we all have meals every day! Incorporate this simple question into your regular mealtimes.
