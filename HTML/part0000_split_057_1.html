Pronunciation note: When you see the letter eur together, you pronounce the sound like yoor.

Cultural note: Do you like fairy tales? Many well known stories have come from French author Charles Perrault. He wrote Cinderella, Sleeping Beauty and Puss in Boots.

Idea! Play ¿Chaud ou Froid? (Hot or Cold?). Play the same way you would play Hot or Cold? But instead of hiding an item, you simply think of one in the room and direct your children toward it by saying chaud or froid. When they find it they have to tell you what it is in French. Then they get a turn!
