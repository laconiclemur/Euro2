Grilled Potatoes cooked in duck fat

Confit potatoes, slowly cooked in delicious duck fat, go beautifully with tête de veau revisitée (see here), among other dishes.

Serves 6–8

8 potatoes, such as Maris Pipers or Roosters

200g duck fat

salt

Peel the potatoes and, using a sharp knife, trim them into cylinder shapes with a flat top and bottom. Cook them briefly on a hot ridged grill until they are nicely marked with the grill lines.

Melt the duck fat in a pan and add the potatoes – they should be submerged in the fat. Cook for about 25 minutes or until tender. Season with a little salt.
