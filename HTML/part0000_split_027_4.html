Almond Ice Cream


Every country has a staple ice cream or, at least, favorite one. This is a Spanish version of the classic icy treat.

Serves: 6

Time: 10 minutes + inactive time

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups blanched almonds
  • ½ cinnamon stick
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tablespoons grated lemon zest
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar


Directions:

1. Finely grind the almonds using a food blender.

2. In a saucepan, combine the cinnamon stick, water, sugar, and zest. Add half the almonds.

3. Heat the mix over high heat and bring to boil, stirring constantly.

4. Reduce heat to medium and add the rest of almonds, continuing to stir. Once the mixture start to boil again, remove from the heat and let cool completely.

5. Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer directions.

6. Remove the ice cream from the freezer 5 minutes before serving.

7. Serve in a dessert bowls.
