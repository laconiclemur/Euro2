Crispy Spice Cookies

 

For the cookies:

·         1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

·         1/2 cup sugar

·         1/2 tsp. baking powder

·         2 Tbsps. pumpkin spice powder or apple pie spice mix

·         1 stick butter

·         1 egg, lightly beaten

·         1 tsp. vanilla

 

For the topping:

·         1/2 stick butter

·         2 Tbsps. milk

·         1/4 cup brown sugar

·         3/4 cup sweetened coconut flakes

·         3/4 cup chopped walnuts

 

In the food processor, pulse flour, sugar, baking powder and spices.  Add the butter and process until it forms coarse crumbs.  Combine the lightly beaten egg with the vanilla, then add to the flour mix and process until a smooth dough ball forms.

 

Preheat the oven to 400° F.  Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Divide the dough into about 30 small balls and flatten them with your finger.

 

For the topping, melt the butter and milk.  Add the sugar, coconut flakes and walnuts.  Stir well and spoon the mixture on top of the cookies.  Bake 9 to 10 minutes.
