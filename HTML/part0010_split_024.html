Vinaigrette aux herbes

Herb dressing

Makes about 500ml

2 free-range egg yolks

1 tsp Dijon mustard

1Â½ tbsp tarragon vinegar

150ml extra virgin olive oil

300ml vegetable oil

1 tbsp each of chives, flatleaf parsley and tarragon, chopped

salt

black pepper

Put the egg yolks in a blender with the mustard, vinegar, salt and pepper. Blend at high speed, slowly adding the olive and vegetable oils, a little at a time. After a third of the oil has been incorporated, add the herbs. Continue to blend, adding the oils a little at a time. The vinaigrette should have the consistency of pouring cream. If the mixture becomes too thick, add a few drops of cold water to the blender.

If you want to remove the herbs, pass the vinaigrette through a fine sieve. This can be kept in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
