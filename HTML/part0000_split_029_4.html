Polvorones


Polvorones are popular Spanish cookies and delicacy served during holidays. You do not have to wait the holidays to prepare and serve.

Serves: 10

Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon brandy
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder


Directions:

1. Cream the butter with granulated and white sugar.

2. Add the eggs one at the time and stir until blended thoroughly.

3. In a separate bowl, whisk the flour, salt, and baking powder.

4. Fold in the butter mix. Mix the dough and transfer onto a floured surface. Roll it out to ¼-inch thick. Cut the cookies using a cookie cutter. Transfer the cookies onto a baking sheet lined with a parchment paper. Bake for 12-15 minutes at 375F.

5. Combine powdered sugar and cinnamon. Dust the cookies while still hot. Serve at room temperature.
