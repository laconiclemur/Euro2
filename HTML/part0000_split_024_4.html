Grilled Pepper and Potato Salad


A grilled sweet peppers and garlic will turn the simple potatoes into an extraordinary side dish.

Serves: 6

Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 red bell peppers, seeded and quartered
  • 2lb. yellow potatoes, halved
  • ½ cup aioli or mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Some olive oil


Directions:

1. Preheat grill to 450F. Brush the peppers with olive oil and grill on skin side for 5 minutes. Flip and grill for 2 minutes more.

2. Let the peppers cool slightly and peel any charred skin. Cut the peppers into a bite size pieces.

3. Meanwhile, boil the potatoes in a pot of salted water until fork tender.

4. Drain and combine with red peppers. In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise and garlic. Stir in the potato mix and sprinkle with parsley.

5. Adjust seasonings before serving.
