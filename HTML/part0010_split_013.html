Jus de veau

veal jus

Makes about 2 litres

1kg veal bones, chopped small

3 shallots, chopped

200ml dry white wine

2.5 litres veal stock (see here)

Preheat the oven to 220°C/Fan 200°C/Gas 7. Put the bones in a roasting pan and roast them in the oven, turning occasionally, until brown all over. Transfer the bones to a deep saucepan.

Brown the shallots in the roasting pan, stirring frequently. Add the wine and stir to loosen all the residue. Boil to reduce by half, then pour into the saucepan with the bones. Add the stock and bring to the boil. Simmer for 45 minutes, skimming occasionally, then strain.
