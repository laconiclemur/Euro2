tomato vinaigrette

{ vinagreta de tomate–catalonia }

This Catalan dressing (vinagreta de tomàquet in Catalan) is wonderful with fish and seafood salads and with boiled or steamed vegetables.

SERVES 4 TO 6

4 to 5 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar or fresh lemon juice

Salt and pepper

2 medium tomatoes or 1 large tomato (about 5 ounces), diced or chopped (see the variations)

With a fork, beat the oil with the vinegar or lemon and salt and pepper to taste in a small bowl, then mix in the tomatoes.

VARIATIONS

• For a sweet-and-sour Andalusian version, add 2 teaspoons honey.

• Add 4 finely chopped scallions or 1 crushed garlic clove plus 1 tablespoon chopped flat-leaf parsley.

• Instead of chopping the tomatoes, cut them crosswise in half and grate them on the large holes of a vegetable grater, leaving the skin behind.
