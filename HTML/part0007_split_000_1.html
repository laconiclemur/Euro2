Vegetables are treated with proper care and respect in the French kitchen. Not only do they partner fish or meat but they can also be the stars of many dishes and the basis of a whole meal. Seasonality is all important. Go to the market in a French town and you will find vegetables that are growing in the region, rather than items imported from many different parts of the world.
