Cheese and eggs make a wonderful marriage, but cheese on its own is truly delicious. No meal in France, however casual, is complete without a little cheese. For me, eggs are the simplest and most satisfying of fast food. They are so versatile, used in everything from the quickest snack to the most elaborate recipes. And eggs are not just an ingredient in French cooking – they can be the focal point of many recipes and have inspired some great classics. There’s nothing as French as a perfect omelette.
